Learning On Your Time

Using the website:

Here are some simple steps to follow in order to utilize this website:

1. First click on a subject tab that corresponds to what class/classes you are looking for. Currently the website has three options to choose from Math, English, and Computer Science.

2.Once a tab is clicked you will be greeted with a drop-down window with several radio buttons that narrow down the subject even more. Select one of the options and click the submit button at the bottom of the page.

3. Once the submit button is clicked another drop-down window will appear with live links associated with the selection you made. If you wish you can click on the links to be redirected to the appropriate pages. If you wish you can repeat the process but choose a different subject, once you click submit the new links will appear.
